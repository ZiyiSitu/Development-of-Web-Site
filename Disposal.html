Disposal of unwanted electronics

Due to state environmental laws, Waste Management is unable to accept TVs, computer monitors or other "e-waste" through our bulky item pick-up program. Please take these items to Waste Management's Sunset Environmental Transfer Station in Irvine (see below) for safe disposal.
